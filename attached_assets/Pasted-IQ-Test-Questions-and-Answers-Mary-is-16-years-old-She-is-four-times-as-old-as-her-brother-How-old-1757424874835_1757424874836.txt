IQ Test Questions and Answers
Mary is 16 years old. She is four times as old as her brother. How old will Mary be when she is twice as old as her brother?
Answer: 24 years old

Which fraction is the biggest? 3/5, 5/8, 1/2, 4/7
Answer: 5/8

A store reduces the price of an item by 20%. By what percentage must the new price be increased to return to the original price?
Answer: 25%

There are 5 machines making 5 parts in 5 minutes. How long does it take 100 machines to make 100 parts?
Answer: 5 minutes

A lake is covered by water lilies that double in area daily. If it takes 48 days for the lilies to cover the whole lake, how long until they cover half the lake?
Answer: 47 days

A car travels to a destination at 40 mph and returns at 60 mph. What is the average speed for the entire trip?
Answer: 48 mph

What comes once in a minute, twice in a moment, but never in a thousand years?
Answer: The letter “M”

Identify the odd word out: Clavichord, Harpsichord, Clarion, Accordion
Answer: Clarion (it’s a type of trumpet, others are keyboard instruments)

Which of the following is not an anagram of "intelligence test"? Testing Elect Line, Testing Clientele, Ties Gentle Client, Let Testing Client
Answer: Let Testing Client

If two people start at the same place and walk 4 meters opposite directions, then both turn left and walk 3 meters, approximately how far apart are they?
Answer: 7.5 meters

Vlad says: "Jeremy always tells the truth." Jeremy says: "I don't always tell the truth." Bushra says: "Jeremy always lies." Who is telling the truth?
Answer: Vlad is telling the truth; Jeremy is lying; Bushra is lying

Which one of these is not a musical instrument? Niloiv, Matban, Thizre, Lamycb
Answer: Matban

Rearrange the letters “EMRUD” to form a valid English word.
Answer: Murder

What is an Orrery?
Answer: A mechanical model of the solar system

Which number fills the sequence? 9, 27, 6, 18, 45, ?, 6, 21, 7
Answer: 27

Which day comes next? Sunday, Monday, Wednesday, Saturday, Wednesday, ?
Answer: Monday

Which word means the same as ESOTERIC?
Answer: Secret

What is a Gigot?
Answer: A leg of mutton

Find the next number: 2, 6, 12, 20, ?
Answer: 30

What object has 13 hearts but no other organs?
Answer: A deck of cards
