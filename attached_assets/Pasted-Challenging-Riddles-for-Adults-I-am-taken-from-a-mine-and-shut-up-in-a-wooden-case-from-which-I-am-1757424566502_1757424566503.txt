Challenging Riddles for Adults
I am taken from a mine, and shut up in a wooden case, from which I am never released, and yet I am used by almost every person. What am I? – Pencil lead

The person who makes it, sells it. The person who buys it never uses it. The person who uses it doesn’t know they are. What is it? – Coffin

I am the beginning of the end, and the end of time and space. I am essential to creation, and I surround every place. What am I? – The letter “E”

The more you have, the less you see. What is it? – Darkness

A man is looking at a picture of someone. His friend asks, “Who is it you are looking at?” The man replies, “Brothers and sisters, I have none. But that man's father is my father's son.” Who was in the picture? – His son

Which weighs more, a pound of feathers or a pound of stones? – Neither

The one who has it doesn’t need it. The one who needs it doesn’t know it. The one who knows it doesn’t want it. What is it? – Money

What has cities, but no houses; forests, but no trees; and rivers, but no water? – Map
